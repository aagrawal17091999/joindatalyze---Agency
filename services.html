Services

Ways we help

Product Analytics

We help you setup product analytics, get insights and impact revenue. On a high level, it’s all about:

  • Creating tracking plan
  • Building reports and dashboards
  • Deep diving to uncover user behavioural insights, and suggesting gaps to work on, low hanging fruits to fix and more.

Data sanity, consolidation & modelling

If you feel your data is broken, let us help you.

If you don’t trust your data → we’ll conduct a data audit for you, and work with you to fix it, and reach a stage where you can start trusting your data again.

If your data is fragmented → we’ll help you unify your data in one place, with proper user mapping, and enable you to visualize the entire user journey.

If you want to build data/ AI models on top of your data for personalized reachouts, any analysis, or want to set up automations → we’ll do that for you.

Growth & Experimentation

We help you with UI/ UX audits, and point out obvious gaps in the product to improve conversion at every step.

We will also work with you to run experiments in order to improve conversions. Additionally, we will work with your product team to identify growth levers, identify competitive advantages that can be used.
