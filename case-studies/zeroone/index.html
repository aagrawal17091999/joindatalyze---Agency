zeroone · Web3 Social

Activation, retention, and analytics readiness for a creator network

We layered experiments and tracking to spark creation, collection, and habit loops.

Challenge: Low artwork upload completion after onboarding

Users finished onboarding but didn’t upload their first artwork.

Solution

  • Optional upload prompt during onboarding to encourage early contribution.
  • Removed unnecessary onboarding screens to reduce friction.
  • Gave free collection tickets so users could collect before uploading.

Result

Activation increased from 21% to 55%; Week 2 retention improved by 15%.

Challenge: Week 2 retention dropped by 26%

Needed clarity on causes across releases, segments, and funnel steps.

Solution

  • Analyzed releases, acquisition segments, device cohorts, and art actions.
  • Identified funnel steps and technical issues causing the decline.

Result

Engineering is rolling out fixes; early tests show retention recovery.

Challenge: Low ongoing engagement and lack of habit loops

Solution

  • Introduced streaks, leaderboards, and tiered rewards to drive daily activity.

Result

Engagement increased by 30% with more consistent collecting and posting.

Challenge: New app launch with no tracking in place

Solution

  • Deep-dived new app flows and edge cases.
  • Created complete tracking plan with event definitions, properties, funnels, and naming conventions.
  • Built implementation guidelines for engineering.

Result

Tracking plan in implementation to deliver full behavioural visibility post-launch.
