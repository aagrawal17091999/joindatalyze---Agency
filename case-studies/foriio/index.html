Foriio · Portfolio Platform

Activation & retention improvements for creative portfolios

Foriio helps designers showcase work; we focused on activation and habit-building.

Challenge: Activation problem

Users signed up but didn’t add their first work due to impulsive signups and confusing upload UX.

Solution

  • Behaviour-driven email nudges reminding users to add work, highlighting ease, and showcasing strong portfolios.
  • Simplified the “Add Work” flow into a clean two-click process with clearer guidance.

Result

Activation rate improved by 8%, exposing more users to the “aha” moment.

Challenge: Retention problem

Users rarely returned after first upload; they forgot about the platform and lacked new reasons to visit.

Solution

  • Timely notifications for new views, activity spikes, and performance updates.
  • New sticky features: portfolio analytics and lightweight blogging to keep users engaged.

Result

Retention improved by 6% through recurring value moments and ongoing engagement.
