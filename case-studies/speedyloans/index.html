Cost-efficient lifecycle marketing with data science

We combined scoring and association mining to send fewer, smarter emails.

Challenge: Millions of emails, rising costs & domain risk

Solution

  • Built a weighted Python scoring model using loan history, engagement, and behaviour signals.
  • Prioritized outreach to users above score thresholds to maximize ROI.

Result

  • Email costs -30%
  • Re-engagement +1% despite reduced volume

Challenge: 100+ attributes with no prioritization

Solution

  • Applied the Apriori algorithm to find high-probability attribute combinations for conversion.
  • Surfaced behavioural sequences and demographic clusters that manual analysis missed.

Result

Sharper targeting and faster analysis now guide ongoing segmentation strategy.
