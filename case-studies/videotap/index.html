Onboarding and upload flows that drive activation

We made the path to first upload obvious and error-free so users reach value fast.

Challenge: High drop-off during onboarding

Unnecessary questions and unclear value prevented users from reaching the dashboard.

Solution

  • Streamlined steps, removed non-essential questions, and reordered screens for speed.
  • Improved value communication with visuals and outcome-first messaging.

Result

Onboarding completion increased from 28% to 80%.

Challenge: Users failing to upload first video

A complex four-step upload, limited guidance, and YouTube errors blocked activation.

Solution

  • Simplified upload from four steps to two with clearer CTAs and hints.
  • Reworked dashboard empty states to prompt first upload.
  • Fixed YouTube import errors for reliability.

Result

Activation increased from 7% to 19% as more users uploaded their first video.
