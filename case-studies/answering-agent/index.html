Call-quality scoring to improve AI outreach

We built clarity around what makes a “good” AI call and how to fix the rest.

Challenge

No visibility into AI call quality; product decisions were guesswork.

Solution

  • Researched call flows, industry benchmarks, and real-world scenarios.
  • Defined metrics for clarity, relevance, latency, task completion, sentiment, and compliance.
  • Built a scoring model that surfaces issues and provides overall quality scores per call.

Result

Teams instantly see poor calls and reasons, enabling faster, targeted AI improvements.
