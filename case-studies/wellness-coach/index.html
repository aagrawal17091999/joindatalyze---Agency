Analytics taxonomy that restores trust

Millions of noisy events needed structure so teams could rely on retention and engagement metrics.

Challenge

Overtracking with inconsistent event names, properties, and governance led to data chaos.

Solution

  • Audited event names, properties, redundancies, and gaps across key workflows.
  • Created a simplified taxonomy with clear conventions, journey categories, and consistent schemas.
  • Removed noisy click events and prioritized meaningful activation, engagement, revenue, and retention signals.

Result

Teams regained confidence in dashboards; decisions, experiments, and planning now rely on clean data.
