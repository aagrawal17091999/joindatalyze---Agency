Sama.io · Coaching SaaS

Driving return engagement through Notes visibility

Sama.io offers personalized coaching; we focused on lifting returns between sessions.

Challenge

Users didn’t return after coaching sessions; the Notes feature correlated with repeat visits but was hidden.

Solution

  • Moved Notes into prime screen real estate and reduced clicks to access.
  • Integrated Notes with upcoming sessions and past conversations for natural reuse.

Result

Return retention lifted by 4% as users reviewed, recorded, and prepared regularly.
