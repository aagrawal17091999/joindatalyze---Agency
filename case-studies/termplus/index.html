TermPlus · Finserv

PostHog setup and unified funnels for growth clarity

We created a complete tracking plan, unified data, and delivered clear dashboards.

Challenge

Incomplete PostHog setup and fragmented product/marketing data blocked visibility.

Solution

  • Audited the product to map journeys, activation, and conversion points.
  • Designed tracking plans with naming conventions and properties, guided implementation, and verified accuracy.
  • Unified datasets into PostHog tied to a single user identity.
  • Built dashboards for product metrics, marketing performance, funnels, and drop-offs.

Result

TermPlus now has full visibility into user journeys and can act quickly on insights.
