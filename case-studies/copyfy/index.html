Full-funnel analytics and conversion lifts for an AI site builder

Copyfy helps founders and creators ship Shopify storefronts with AI. We built the analytics foundation and removed onboarding friction so the team could move quickly with trustworthy data.

Challenge: No reliable analytics & limited insight

They needed Mixpanel end-to-end, a unified identity graph, and trusted dashboards.

  • No clarity on who users were, why they stayed or churned, and which features created friction.
  • Fragmented product and marketing data without a single user view.

Solution

  • Designed a clean event taxonomy across activation, engagement, retention, and new releases.
  • Guided engineering on accurate instrumentation and validated live events for data quality.
  • Unified multiple datasets into one user identity to enable behaviour-based segmentation.
  • Built Mixpanel dashboards to track product performance, feature adoption, drop-offs, journeys, and retention.
  • Provided ongoing insights and product recommendations tied to behavioural trends.

Result

  • Faster decision-making with clear visibility into users, channels, and long-term stickiness.
  • Personalized reactivation strategies powered by unified data.
  • Leadership now runs on trusted numbers instead of guesswork.

Challenge: Low website-to-signup conversion

Visitors abandoned onboarding due to cluttered UI, misaligned expectations, and extra steps.

Solution

  • Simplified UI and copy to reduce cognitive load.
  • Removed redundant steps and consolidated actions to keep momentum.
  • Restructured flow to feel faster and aligned with AI-builder expectations.
  • Ran iterative experiments on layout, messaging, and CTAs.

Result

Signup conversion increased from 30% to 61%, more than doubling users entering the product.
