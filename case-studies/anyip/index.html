Marketing analytics ownership for clear ROI

We cleaned data, structured dashboards, and enabled day-to-day channel decisions.

Challenge

Messy, inconsistent data and unclear dashboards made CAC and channel performance hard to measure.

Solution

  • Cleaned and standardized datasets across channels with aligned tracking.
  • Built dashboards for channel performance, CAC/ROI trends, funnels, and campaign breakdowns.
  • Enabled ongoing visibility so budgets and experiments could be directed by data.

Result

Marketing performance improved, CAC decreased, and decisions now rely on structured reporting.
