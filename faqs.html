FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Datalyze?

Datalyze is an analytics and growth partner. We help you set up your data correctly, understand how users behave, and use those insights to run experiments and improve revenue.

How fast can you get started?

We can usually start within a day once you decide to work with us.

Who will I work with?

You’ll work with a small, focused team based on your needs. This may include a product analyst, analytics engineer, growth strategist, and developer. There are no unnecessary layers.

How is pricing structured?

Pricing is either one-time or monthly, depending on the scope and duration of the work.

What do you need from our team?

We may need light developer support to implement tracking, add events, or help with data foundations for modeling.

What does a typical engagement look like?

We usually have at least one call per week to review progress, share insights, and align on priorities for the coming week.
